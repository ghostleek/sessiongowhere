session go where?

sharing street subcultures in Singapore

Sessiongowhere is an initiative by Elevate The Streets to identify spots where street subcultural practioners in Singapore can be found. We are starting with the spots that our core team members practice as well as an open schedule where eventually, once social distancing measures relax, people interested in these art forms can come and join us. Crews and artists who believe in our vision to create a more cohesive street subcultural community in Singapore can also reach out to us through this form.
